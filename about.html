About Our System

The Real-Time Disaster Alert System is a dynamic and responsive platform designed to empower users during natural calamities. By harnessing the power of geolocation, AI-driven severity analysis, and real-time map integration, this system provides critical alerts with precision and clarity.

Why We Built This

Natural disasters strike without warning, often leaving people unprepared. Our goal is to build a system that not only notifies users in real-time but also helps them make informed decisions through custom safety tips, AI-generated risk levels, and a visually engaging experience.

Key Features

  • Interactive map with live disaster alerts
  • Location-based warnings using geolocation
  • AI classification for disaster severity
  • In-app notifications & user safety tips
  • Optional crowdsourced reporting module

Our Vision

We believe technology should act as a life-saving companion in times of need. Through this project, we aim to combine simplicity, speed, and smart design to help communities stay informed, prepared, and safe.
